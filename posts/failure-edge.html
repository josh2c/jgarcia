Failure's Edge: Where Growth Meets Risk

The edge of failure is where growth lives. It's that space between what you know and what you're reaching for. Every stumble there teaches something new, if you're willing to learn. The trick isn't avoiding falls - it's falling better.

Risk and reward dance together here. Playing it safe means staying the same, while each step into uncertainty brings both danger and possibility. The path forward isn't always clear, but that's exactly why it's worth taking.
