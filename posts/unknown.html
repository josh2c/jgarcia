The Unknown: Why Fear It When It's All We've Got?

The unknown's every start, blank slate, no script. It's tense, yeah. But it's alive, wins hide there. Every breakthrough began with a step into the dark, every innovation with a question no one dared ask.

We build walls against uncertainty, stack plans like bricks, but the unknown always finds a way in. It's not the enemy, it's the canvas. The blank space where new ideas take shape, where old rules don't apply.

Fear of the unknown is natural, but it's also a choice. We can let it paralyze us, or we can let it propel us forward. The unknown isn't a void to avoid, it's the frontier where the future is being written.
