Simplicity: The Art of Less

Simple's hard, that's the trick. We pile on features, stack up options, thinking more means better. But clean design? That's power. Strip it down, find what matters, cut what doesn't. Each piece should earn its spot.

Less isn't lazy, it's brave. Saying no to extras, keeping what works. Why complicate what could flow? What happens when you trust the basics? Pure function has its own beauty, and it lasts.

What could you strip away today?
