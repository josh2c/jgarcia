Limits of Control: Can You Steer What You Start?

Launch something new, watch it grow, see it change. Your vision meets the world, and the world pushes back. Some shifts you plan, others just happen. The trick isn't perfect control, it's knowing when to guide and when to let go.

Every project takes its own shape once it's out there. Users find new ways, teams add their touch, markets pull different directions. What matters more: your first dream or what it becomes? Maybe the best control is knowing its limits.
