AI and Coding: The New Pair Programming

The landscape of software development is shifting. AI isn't just a tool anymore; it's becoming a partner in the coding process. But what does this mean for developers, and how do we navigate this new reality?

AI can write code, suggest improvements, and even debug issues. But it's not replacing developers – it's augmenting them. The real skill now is knowing how to work with AI, how to ask the right questions, and how to validate and refine its output.

The future of coding isn't about choosing between human or AI – it's about finding the right balance. It's about leveraging AI to handle the repetitive tasks while focusing human creativity on solving complex problems and creating innovative solutions.
