Reality's Bend: What Happens When Plans Meet Life?

Plans are perfect until they meet reality. That's when the interesting part begins. Your carefully drawn map hits unexpected terrain, and suddenly you're navigating by instinct as much as intention.

The beauty lies in this collision - between what you imagined and what actually unfolds. Sometimes the detours lead to better destinations. The key is staying flexible while keeping your core direction. Reality doesn't break good plans, it reshapes them into something more resilient.
