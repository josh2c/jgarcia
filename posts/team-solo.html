Walking the Path: Why a Team Beats Going Solo

No one's built to walk every path alone, it's a truth that hits hard. Solo runs burn bright, all-nighters, pure grit, chasing the end. But limits creep in, energy fades, blind spots grow. A team flips that. People bring what one can't, new eyes, extra hands.

Shared goals hit different. When minds click, work flows, ideas spark. Sure, solo's got its moments, pure focus, quick moves. But teams? They build bigger, last longer, see further. Why cap your climb when others lift you higher?
