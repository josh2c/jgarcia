Small Wins: Why Do They Matter More Than We Think?

Big goals grab attention, but small wins pile up silently. A quick fix, a nod from someone who gets it, a step that holds. They don't scream, yet they're the foundation. Chasing the peak's fine, but these keep the flame alive.

Every giant stride began with a stumble forward. We often skip over them, though they're the steady fuel. A spark today can ignite something huge tomorrow, patience makes it gold. Those little lifts aren't just noise, they're the heartbeat of progress.
